Privacy Policy

Discord Server Management Bot

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Discord Server Management Bot ("the Bot", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you invite the Bot to your Discord server ("the Server").

By using the Bot, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Server Configuration Data

  • Server ID (guild ID)
  • Channel IDs (for logging, welcome, and join-to-create channels)
  • Role IDs (for auto-role assignments)
  • Custom message templates (welcome/goodbye messages)

User Data

  • User IDs (for voice channel ownership and auto-role assignments)
  • Voice channel association data (linking users to their created channels)
  • Custom voice channel preferences (names and settings)

Activity Data

  • Message edit and deletion logs (when logging is enabled)
  • Member join and leave events (when logging is enabled)
  • Voice channel creation and deletion events

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for providing and improving the Bot's functionality:

  • To create and manage join-to-create voice channels
  • To assign auto-roles to new members
  • To send welcome and goodbye messages
  • To maintain server-specific configurations
  • To log server events when logging features are enabled
  • To maintain user preferences for voice channel settings

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in PostgreSQL databases hosted on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only. We regularly review our security practices to ensure continued protection of your data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforceable governmental requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with appropriate notice)

Discord Inc. receives information directly from its platform as part of the API integration, which is governed by Discord's own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Server configuration data is retained until the Bot is removed from your server
  • User association data is retained while you remain on servers where the Bot is active
  • Log data is retained according to server administrator preferences

You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us through the methods described in Section 8.

7. Third-Party Services

This Bot operates on the Discord platform. Your use of the Bot is subject to Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for Discord's data practices.

Our hosting provider and database services may have access to data as necessary for infrastructure operation, but only under strict confidentiality agreements.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Right to request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Right to request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Right to request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Right to request transfer of data to another party

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the methods described below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Bot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • Opening an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Direct messaging the bot administrators
